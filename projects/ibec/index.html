What We Do

Analysis of Global Markets

Committee members will follow major global events and analyze them to determine how markets are likely to move.

Regional Committees

Members become analysts for committees that represent the markets of particular world regions (Latin America, East Asia, Europe, etc). Committees will present their findings to the club to inform investment decisions.
