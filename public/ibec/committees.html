Our Committees

Learn about what our committees do!

North American Committee

Analyzes major events and general market movements within the USA and Canada. Current events that will impact analysis will be the upcoming presidential elections, the recent Fed interest rate reduction, and the growth of American fintech startups.

Latin American Committee

Analyzes major events and general market movements within Latin America (Mexico, Central America, and South America). Analysis will be structured around developments in administration change and their effects on the economies in Central and South America, especially those of Mexico, Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, Chile, and Uruguay.

European Committee

Analyzes major events and general market movements within Europe, both EU and non-EU members. Focus will be on the British stock market, EU policies and regulations, and current conflicts.

East Asian Committee

Analyzes major events and general market movements primarily within Japan, China, South Korea, and Taiwan. Japan’s Nikkei, Taiwan’s semiconductor industry, and China’s massive volume of trade with the US are significant items.

South Asian Committee

Analyzes major events and general market movements primarily within India, though attention will also be directed towards Pakistan, Sri Lanka, and other countries in the region. India’s role as an increasingly important trade partner of the US and Europe will be given a great deal of focus.

Middle Eastern Committee

Analyzes events occuring on the Near East, from Syria and Lebanon to the Arabian Peninsula to Iran. Especially important in this committee is OPEC, regional conflicts, and strained relations with the US.

Corporate Outreach Committee

Responsible for connecting IBEC to corporations to initiate partnerships, promote career opportunities, and secure funding. Strategically utilizing various methods of outreach and networking to ensure these will be essential to the role.
