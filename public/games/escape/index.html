Escape Game

üéØ How to Play

Goal: Move the red block to the bottom exit!

  • Red block: This is your target piece - get it to the gray exit area at the bottom
  • Yellow blocks: Larger obstacle pieces that block your path
  • Blue blocks: Smaller pieces that can help create space

Controls:

  • Click and drag any piece to move it
  • Pieces can only move into empty spaces
  • Think strategically about which pieces to move first!

Tips: Sometimes you need to move other pieces out of the way before you can move the red block to the exit.
