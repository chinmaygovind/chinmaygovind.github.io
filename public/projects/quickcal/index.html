Project Overview

QuickCal is a Chrome extension that lets you add events to your Google Calendar in seconds, from anywhere on the web. It recognizes dates and times in natural language, so you can create events with just a few clicks.

Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone who wants to stay organized without leaving their workflow.

How it works
  1. Highlight any text on a webpage that represents an event description.
  2. Right click the text and click the "Add to Calendar" option in the toolbar.
  3. QuickCal will automatically detect the event details and prompt you to confirm.

Powered by Gemini AI for natural language event parsing.
