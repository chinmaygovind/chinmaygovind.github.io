ROBOT TOUR
Science Olympiad Autonomous Robotics Project
BUILD

Meet the Robot

  • Chassis: Custom 3D Printed for durability and modularity.
  • Brains: Arduino-based, programmed in C++.
  • Sensors: Motor Encoders + MPU6050 Gyroscope.
  • Power: 6 AA+ Batteries.
  • Performance: The robot can navigate 10+ meter-long paths in mazes, avoiding barriers and getting within inches of the target.
EVENT

What is Robot Tour?

In this event, teams design, build, program, and test one Robotic Vehicle to navigate a track to reach a target at a set amount of time as accurately and efficiently as possible.
